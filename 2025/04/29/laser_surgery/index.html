近视激光手术资料整理

Date: April 29, 2025  Tuesday Category: example

术后效果好不好，有没有并发症，和你的术前检查完的眼部情况有很大关系。检查完符合手术要求的：风险小。

手术方式选择：

  • 全飞秒：在所有条件都满足时，首选全飞秒。
  • 半飞秒：适合高散光，切口较大，眼干更严重，终生有角膜瓣移位风险。
  • 晶体植入（ICL）：风险极高，不要轻信“可逆性”的说法。

  1. 角膜厚度：参考范围：500～550um 最低不低于480um，最好500um以上，术后不低于400um

  2. 角膜曲率： 正常范围：39D～46D 术后风险：曲率超出范围可能会存在圆锥角膜，视力回退的风险！

  3. 暗瞳值： 正常标准<7mm 手术风险：超过6.8mm不建议全飞秒，超过8mm不建议近视手术，防止术后可能出现眩光

  4. 干眼标准：But/泪液分泌值＞10秒，且睑板腺正常。手术风险：干眼患者不适合半飞秒，but低于5正常范围视为严重干眼，不建议进行激光类手术

  5. Kappa角： 正常范围：0~5 手术风险:Kappa大于0.2度不建议进行全飞秒，可能会导致手术偏心切割

  6. 眼压： 正常范围：10～21mmHg 手术风险：双眼眼压差超过5mmHg可能提示青光眼风险，不建议进行晶体植入

  7. 前房深度与前房角 前房深度标准:>2.8mm 前房角标准:>35 手术风险:前房深度和前房角的大小决定能否进行晶体植入。虽然部分医生能够在前房深度 2.7mm的情况下进行手术，但手术风险较高

  8. 内皮细胞数： 最低标准：不低于2000/mm2 手术风险：晶体植入的基本要求，内皮细胞会随着年龄增长而较少，数量下降一定程度，可能会带来风险

📝 Pre-Surgery Checklist （术前准备清单）ᡣ𐭩

✅ 1. Before the pre-operative consultationᡣ𐭩

  • Stop wearing contact lenses:
    • Soft lenses: at least 5 days before the consultation
    • Hard lenses / RGP: at least 2 weeks before
  • Prepare a list of medical history and current medications.
  • Avoid eye makeup, lotion, or perfume on the day of your consultation.

✅ 2. Before the surgery (1–2 weeks prior)ᡣ𐭩

  • Confirm your surgery type with the doctor (e.g., ZYOPTIX FEMTO ELITE, ZYOPTIX ELITE, or EPI-LASIK ELITE).
  • Arrange for a friend or taxi to accompany you on surgery day—you should not drive.
  • Buy prescribed eye drops in advance (antibiotic, steroid, artificial tears).
  • Avoid alcohol and caffeine 24 hours before the procedure.

✅ 3. On the day of surgeryᡣ𐭩

  • Wear comfortable clothing, no makeup, no perfume or hairspray.
  • Bring sunglasses (UV protection!) for after surgery.
  • Bring your insurance card, ID, and any payment documentation.

🛟 Post-Surgery Care Instructions （术后护理指南）ᡣ𐭩

👁️ First 24–72 hoursᡣ𐭩

  • Do not rub your eyes!
  • Rest as much as possible. Keep eyes closed when possible.
  • Use all prescribed eye drops on schedule.
  • Wear eye shields or sleep goggles while sleeping.
  • Avoid all screens (phones, laptops, TVs) for at least 24 hours.

😎 First weekᡣ𐭩

  • Avoid makeup, creams, and lotions near the eyes.
  • Avoid smoking areas, dusty or windy environments.
  • No swimming, saunas, or strenuous sports.
  • Avoid washing hair in a way that water may enter the eyes.
  • Wear sunglasses outdoors even on cloudy days.

📅 First monthᡣ𐭩

  • Resume normal screen use gradually, depending on eye comfort.
  • Continue to avoid contact sports and direct eye trauma.
  • Use artificial tears regularly if experiencing dry eye.
  • Attend all follow-up appointments as scheduled.

✨ Bonus Tipsᡣ𐭩

  • Set alarms/reminders for eye drop times.
  • Store eye drops in the fridge—they feel more soothing cold.
  • Stay hydrated and eat a diet rich in omega-3s for recovery.
  • Track symptoms in a small notebook to report at follow-ups.
